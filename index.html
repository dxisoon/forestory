Forest Insights

Malaysian Forest

The Malaysian forest is home to one of the world's oldest and most biodiverse rainforests. Spanning over centuries, it hosts unique species and plays a crucial role in carbon sequestration.

World Forest

World forests cover about 31% of the Earth's land area. They are vital for global ecosystems, supporting biodiversity and regulating the Earth's climate.
